Hollis Construction Company Limited began as a general works contractor in 2005 under the name of ‘Hollis Concept Ventures Limited’. Over the years, the company has undertaken many challenging projects and accumulated skills, know-how and experiences in design and building solutions, Project Management Services, Building Trades and related engineering works. Today, Hollis Construction Company Limited takes on the role of main contractor for small to medium size projects and performs project management services to coordinate specialist trades for industrial/commercial projects. We also provide design inputs and engineering solutions as value-added services to our clients.

The major areas of specialisation in Hollis Concept Ventures include the fabrication of:
• All kinds of frameless colours stainless steel
• All kinds of Mill Steel Projects
• All kinds of Aluminium profile project
• All kinds of coless shower cubicle project
• All kinds of wooden project
• All kinds of public toiled cubic partitioning project and general renovation of building projects.

Company Profile

Hollis Concept Ventures was incorporated in December 6th, 2005 in Nigeria as an indigenous interior finishing and exterior building engineering, management and maintenance services company under the Companies and Allied matters Decree. The firm has put in over one decade of corporate experience in the industry. The firm aims at providing high standard services to its clients. We always seek to develop and apply new knowledge and innovation techniques where these may benefit the clients and the industry as a whole. The board of the firm is headed by a professional with over 20years experience, equipped with a viable human resource team of qualified professionals who have individually and collectively undertaken works in interior design, supply, installation and building maintenance contract.

Our Project management and execution philosophy is to:
• Create detail schedule and resources plan to meet client’s project objective.
• Communicate clearly with all project stakeholders.
• Track project progress and fine tune deviations.
• Supervise closely on quality of work done.
• Complete and commission the project on time.

With a decade of corporate experience in the industry, we deliver high standard services to our clients.
